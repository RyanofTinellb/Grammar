Honorifics

The suffix -fi can be added to abstract and inanimate nouns to show deference.

Honorifics for people consist of a word prefixed to the person’s name. The general honorific is gusu (person).

The honorific can be a job title.

hisubaRa’ani (Administrator Ryan)
