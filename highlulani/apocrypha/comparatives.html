Comparatives

The comparative marker is an adposition su z than placed on the noun being compared against.

For “little more”, the qualifier tuci eC half-do is affixed to the verb.

For “a lot more”, the qualifier is rali rL intensely.

Equalatives

The equalative marker is ka k as, an adposition.

Superlative

The superlative marker, contrary to the other two, is a particle piba Pb placed before the verb.
