Negative Complements

Use of cu’i yi no one to fill the object argument in a sentence is avoided where possible, thus verbs have a negative complement, i.e.: a syntactically positive word with a semantically negative meaning.

These complements can form compounds with the noun.

When using such an object, the sentence can use either positive or negative auxiliaries without changing the meaning.
