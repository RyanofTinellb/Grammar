Gerunds

Some verb phrases can be changed into nouns describing the activity.

Hubaru naqa.

.Vbw / nq.

Hubaru naqa.

breathe-gergerund necessary

“Breathing is necessary”.

Mika ru pajaha kudasiruqa dasu.

.Mk / w / pjh / >dSwq / dz .

Mika ru pajaha ku-dasi-ru-qa dasu.

Some_group prs;gnopresent gnomic datdative-eat-gergerund-gengenitive meat offend

”Eating meat offends some people.”
