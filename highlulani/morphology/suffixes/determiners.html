Determiners

A determiner can be used to narrow the reference of a noun. They appear at the end of a noun phrase.

cama usual

nisa only

li other

sabufi different

Quantity

These are used to answer ”how many?”

mimu’i some

lumi several

raqi more

’uhu many

takki many

Choice

There are two sets of determiners that are both used to select a part from the whole. One group is used with count nouns, the other with mass nouns.

Count Nouns

These refer to particular members of a group.

ca only

fi’atu certain

tapa next

cari remaining

qafa last

Mass Nouns

These refer to sections of a substance.

cari remaining

sukki entire

Degree

These mark the degree to which the noun matches its referent.

nata complete

naci somewhat

ji’a not
