A Guide to the Tinellbian Languages

This is a grammar of the languages of Tinellb. All these languages descend from a single common ancestor, High Lulani. Lulani and its descendants were taken into each of the Ptokan worlds by the Guozu.

  • High Lulani: WlN

    • Created by Queen Loren on Mala Ptokonoi.
